## Common Controls Used in AutoBrowser
- `FluentWindow` - themed window with title bar support
- `TitleBar` - title bar control
- `ToggleSwitch` - on/off toggle control
- `SymbolIcon` - fluent icon control
- `Button`, `ListView`, `GridView`, `GridViewColumn` - themed standard controls
